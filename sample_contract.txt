SERVICE AGREEMENT

1. The Vendor shall deliver software services within 30 days of signing.

2. The Client shall pay ₹50,000 upon successful completion of the project.

3. The Vendor shall indemnify and hold harmless the Client against all losses, damages, or liabilities.

4. Either party may terminate this agreement at any time with 7 days written notice.

5. A penalty of ₹10,000 per day shall apply for any delay in delivery.

6. This agreement will automatically renew every year unless cancelled 30 days before expiry.

7. The Vendor shall not work with any competing company during the contract term (non-compete).

8. All intellectual property developed under this agreement shall belong exclusively to the Client.

9. The agreement shall be governed by the laws of India and disputes will be resolved through arbitration.

10. Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of business information.
